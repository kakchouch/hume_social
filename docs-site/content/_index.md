Twitter without the noise. Wikipedia with original research.

Hume Social is a platform for structured intellectual discourse where quality emerges from community validation rather than centralized moderation.

## Project Aim and Philosophy

- Quality over Quantity: every contribution follows a rigorous structure.
- Community Validation: quality emerges from distributed peer review.
- Progressive Engagement: users earn capabilities through demonstrated competence.
- Structured Thinking: arguments expose facts, premises, conclusions, and limits.

## Core Workflow

1. Create structured mini-theses.
2. Validate claims through community tagging and editorial review.
3. Build contributor reputation through proven quality.
4. Discover high-rigor content through feed ranking.
